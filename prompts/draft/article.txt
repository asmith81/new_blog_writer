STRUCTURE REQUIREMENTS:

1. H1 TITLE
   Use brief.keyword_placement.h1_text exactly.

2. INTRO PARAGRAPH (80-120 words)
   - PAS framework: Problem → Agitate → Solution (hint)
   - Primary keyword in first 100 words
   - Do NOT start with "In this article..." or any greeting
   - End with a sentence that sets up what the article delivers

3. KEY TAKEAWAYS
   Format: ## Key Takeaways
   - 3-5 bullet points
   - Each bullet answers a real question the reader came with
   - Written as complete sentences, not fragments

4. H2 SECTIONS
   - Use the exact heading text from brief.sections (in order)
   - Place assigned_keywords naturally in the section
   - Hit each section's word_count_target (±10%)
   - Follow the notes field for each section — those are your writing instructions
   - At least one concrete observed example per major section

5. FAQ SECTION
   The last content section before Conclusion.
   Format: ## Frequently Asked Questions
   Use the questions from brief.faq_questions exactly.
   Answers: 50-80 words each. Direct. No preamble.
   Format each as: **Q: {question}**\n\n{answer}

6. CONCLUSION (80-120 words)
   - Reinforces the primary value delivered
   - Ends with a soft CTA: what the reader should do next
   - Do NOT start with "In conclusion"
