Your job is to produce a complete, publish-ready content brief for a single blog article. The brief drives every downstream step: keyword placement, article structure, image generation, and internal linking.

ARTICLE TO PLAN:
- Topic: $topic
- Article type: $article_type
- FG4B brand input (use these themes and links if provided): $fg4b_input_json
- Existing published posts for internal linking: $content_index_json

---

VALID ARTICLE TYPES AND THEIR WORD COUNT RANGES:
- how-to: 1600-2000 words, 2-3 images
- guide: 2200-2800 words, 3-4 images
- list: 1400-1800 words, 2-3 images
- comparison: 1600-2000 words, 1-2 images

VALID CATEGORIES (use one of these exactly):
- Business Growth & Scaling Strategies
- Financial Management & Profitability
- Operations & Workflow Optimization
- Technology & Digital Tools
- Documentation & Record Keeping
- Field & Crew Management

IMAGE TYPES: cover, hero, before-after, over-the-shoulder, process
IMAGE STYLES: graphic-art, photo-real
- Cover image: always graphic-art
- Body images: graphic-art or photo-real depending on what fits

KEYWORD STRATEGY RULES:
- Primary keyword: exact phrase a contractor would search. Include local or role modifier if natural ("for contractors", "contractor", etc.). 30-60 chars.
- Semantic keywords: 4-6 closely related phrases, different angles on the same intent
- LSI keywords: 4-6 contextually related terms — industry terminology, process names, tool names
- meta_title: primary keyword in first 3 words, 50-60 chars exactly
- meta_description: 150-160 chars, primary keyword present, ends with a period, answers the search intent
- h1_text: same as meta_title or a close natural variant, 50-60 chars
- intro_keywords: 2-3 keywords to place in the first 100 words
- image_alt_map: object mapping each image_id to its alt text. Cover alt text must include primary keyword.

SECTION RULES:
- 4-7 H2 sections for how-to/guide, 5-8 for list, 3-5 for comparison
- Include one FAQ section (level 2) and one Conclusion section (level 2) at the end
- Each section gets assigned_keywords (1-2 per section from semantic/LSI lists), a word_count_target (integer), and notes (writing guidance, specific points to cover)
- word_count_target values must sum to total_word_count_target (within 5%)
- Include H3 subsections (level 3) only when a how-to step or list item genuinely needs sub-steps

IMAGE BRIEF RULES:
- First image is always the cover (image_id: "cover", image_type: "cover", style: "graphic-art", placement_after: "")
- Body images: image_id is "body-1", "body-2", etc.
- placement_after: the exact heading text of the H2 section the image should appear after
- dalle_prompt: detailed DALL-E 3 prompt. No text in the image. Contractor/job site context. Cinematic, professional quality.
- alt_text: 10-15 words, describes image, includes assigned keyword where natural
- caption: 1-2 sentences for humans — adds context not in the surrounding text

INTERNAL LINK RULES:
- 2-4 internal links max. Only link to posts that exist in the content_index_json posts array.
- If no relevant posts exist, return an empty array.
- anchor_text: 2-5 words, descriptive, fits naturally in a sentence
- placement_section: the heading text of the section where the link fits best

EXTERNAL LINK:
- 1 dofollow link to a high-authority source (government agency, major trade publication, or university research)
- Must support a specific claim in the article — do not invent the source
- placement_section: heading text of the section where it fits

FG4B BLOCK:
- fg4b_block_placement: heading text of the H2 section after which the FG4B callout block should be inserted
- Choose the section where an observed business example adds the most value
